Community

Weld is an open-source project. Contributions and participation is welcome.

User forum

The Weld user forum is intended for developers who use Weld to build their applications.

IRC Chat

The #weld-dev IRC channel hosted on irc.freenode.net focuses on Weld development discussion. The conversation is logged and the log is available at http://transcripts.jboss.org

Mailing list

Discussions related to development of Weld also takes place in the weld-dev mailing list

Reporting a bug

Bug reports and feature requests can be reported in the project JIRA
